== Geography ==
[[Image:LAMap-doton-Pittsford.png|right|Location of Pittsford, Louisiana]]

Pittsford is located at 27&amp;deg;48'14&quot; North, 89&amp;deg;4'35&quot; West (27.819411, -89.081039){{GR|1}}.

According to the [[United States Census Bureau]], the CDP has a total area of 23.3 [[square kilometer|km&amp;sup2;]] (9.0 [[square mile|mi&amp;sup2;]]).  21.6 km&amp;sup2; (8.4 mi&amp;sup2;) of it is land and 0.2 km&amp;sup2; (0.1 mi&amp;sup2;) of it is water.  The total area is 0.79% water.

== Demographics ==
As of the [[census]][[Geographic references#2|&lt;sup&gt;2&lt;/sup&gt;]] of [[2000]], there are 14,422 people, 5,586 households, and 3,687 families residing in the town.  The [[population density]] is 39.5/km&amp;sup2; (1,122.9/mi&amp;sup2;).  There are 5,672 housing units at an average density of 252.3/km&amp;sup2; (652.0/mi&amp;sup2;).  The racial makeup of the borough is 97.60% [[White (U.S. Census)|White]], 0.91% [[African American (U.S. Census)|African American]], 0.14% [[Native American (U.S. Census)|Native American]],  0.19% [[Asian (U.S. Census)|Asian]], 0.02% [[Pacific Islander (U.S. Census)|Pacific Islander]], 0.32% from [[Race (U.S. Census)|other races]], and 0.64% from two or more races.  1.32% of the population are [[Hispanic (U.S. Census)|Hispanic]] or [[Latino (U.S. Census)|Latino]] of any race.

There are 2,950 households out of which 37.9% have children under the age of 18 living with them, 48.9% are [[Marriage|married couples]] living together, 13.5% have a female householder with no husband present, and 32.9% are non-families. 29.5% of all households are made up of individuals and 15.3% have someone living alone who is 65 years of age or older.  The average household size is 2.42 and the average family size is 3.05.

In the village the population is spread out with 28.3% under the age of 18, 4.8% from 18 to 24, 21.1% from 25 to 44, 18.7% from 45 to 64, and 28.7% who are 65 years of age or older.  The median age is 43 years.  For every 100 females there are 92.4 males.  For every 100 females age 18 and over, there are 87.9 males.

The median income for a household in the village is $38,929, and the median income for a family is $54,044. Males have a median income of $35,833 versus $23,303 for females. The [[per capita income]] for the village is $20,015.  12.0% of the population and 10.8% of families are below the [[poverty line]].  Out of the total population, 11.2% of those under the age of 18 and 27.5% of those 65 and older are living below the poverty line.
