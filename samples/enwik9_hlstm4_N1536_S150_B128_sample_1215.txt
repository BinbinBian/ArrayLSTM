== Demographics ==
As of the [[census]]{{GR|2}} of [[2000]], there are 354,785 people, 15,439 households, and 8,970 families residing in the city.  The [[population density]] is 213.1/km&amp;sup2; (549.5/mi&amp;sup2;).  There are 17,706 housing units at an average density of 82.4/km&amp;sup2; (214.1/mi&amp;sup2;).  The racial makeup of the city is 79.74% [[White (U.S. Census)|White]], 10.53% [[African American (U.S. Census)|African American]], 0.29% [[Native American (U.S. Census)|Native American]], 1.05% [[Asian (U.S. Census)|Asian]], 0.03% [[Pacific Islander (U.S. Census)|Pacific Islander]], 0.00% from [[Race (U.S. Census)|other races]], and 0.69% from two or more races.  0.84% of the population are [ Hispanic (U.S. Census)|Hispanic]] or [[Latino (U.S. Census)|Latino]] of any race.

There are 31,384 households out of which 37.8% have children under the age of 18 living with them, 68.2% are [[Marriage|married couples]] living together, 3.1% have a female householder with no husband present, and 22.1% are non-families. 16.7% of all households are made up of individuals and 10.7% have someone living alone who is 65 years of age or older.  The average household size is 2.83 and the average family size is 3.19.

In the CDP the population is spread out with 29.7% under the age of 18, 7.4% from 18 to 24, 31.2% from 25 to 44, 24.4% from 45 to 64, and 8.8% who are 65 years of age or older.  The median age is 35 years.  For every 100 females there are 96.0 males.  For every 100 females age 18 and over, there are 99.0 males.

The median income for a household in the city is $49,431, and the median income for a family is $54,009. Males have a median income of $42,505 versus $25,411 for females.
