== Demographics ==
As of the [[census]][[Geographic references#2|&lt;sup&gt;2&lt;/sup&gt;]] of [[2000]], there are 941 people, 303 households, and 252 families residing in the CDP.  The [[population density]] is 52.5/km&amp;sup2; (120.7/mi&amp;sup2;).  There are 98 housing units at an average density of 5.4/km&amp;sup2; (14.3/mi&amp;sup2;).  The racial makeup of the CDP is 76.91% [[White (U.S. Census)|White]], 0.26% [[African American (U.S. Census)|African American]], 21.97% [[Native American (U.S. Census)|Native American]], 7.25% [[Asian (U.S. Census)|Asian]], 21.11% [[Pacific Islander (U.S. Census)|Pacific Islander]], 0.29% from [[Race (U.S. Census)|other races]], and 0.56% from two or more races.  7.97% of the population are [[Hispanic (U.S. Census)|Hispanic]] or [[Latino (U.S. Census)|Latino]] of any race.

There are 93 households out of which 23.9% have children under the age of 18 living with them, 73.1% are [[Marriage|married couples]] living together, 12.5% have a female householder with no husband present, and 19.3% are non-families. 24.0% of all households are made up of individuals and 4.4% have someone living alone who is 65 years of age or older.  The average household size is 2.25 and the average family size is 2.68.

In the town the population is spread out with 19.1% under the age of 18, 6.9% from 18 to 24, 24.2% from 25 to 44, 28.6% from 45 to 64, and 6.8% who are 65 years of age or older. The median age is 39 years.  For every 100 females there are 94.9 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there are 85.2 males.

The median income for a household in the town is $26,627, and the median income for a family is $32,250. Males have a median income of $17,250 versus $17,063 for females. The [[per capita income]] for the borough is $20,264.  23.4% of the population and 9.2% of families are below the [[poverty line]].  Out of the total population, 3.4% of those under the age of 18 and 0.0% of those 65 and older are living below the poverty line.
